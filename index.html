About Me

I'm always curious about everything, so I like to consider myself a learner of everything. I am a techy in the most all encompassing way possible; if something requires electricity, I'm automatically an instant fan of it. I've always been surrounded by technology, spent my highschool years building/programming robots, and went on to study electrical and computer engineering at Dalhousie University. I am currently in the process of becoming a full stack web developer, but am also interested in also pursuing cryptography, machine learning, and neural networking. But outside of all that geeky stuff, I like to try and produce my own music (typically hiphop), and also I'm an avid amatuer photographer and graphic designer.
